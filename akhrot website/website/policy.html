Shipping, Billing & Returns Policy

Shipping

Shipping Prices

Please see the "Shipping" tab on the individual product page for current shipping pricing for that product.

Delivery Guarantee

We guarantee delivery to the address you provide, as long as it complies with our shipping policies and is accurate. Please verify all addresses and recipient availability before ordering. For assistance, call 0093209093.

Delivery to Hotels, Hospitals, and Campuses

We do not recommend sending gifts to hospitals, hotels, or campuses due to delivery complications. Please confirm their delivery policies before ordering, as Akhrot is not responsible for delayed or rejected deliveries.

Delivery Details

  • Orders placed after 12:00 PM will be processed the next business day.
  • Organic products ship Monday-Wednesday only.
  • Highly perishable items ordered after Wednesday noon ship the following Monday.
  • During busy holiday seasons, delivery dates are not guaranteed due to high demand.
  • Monthly subscription deliveries depend on harvest schedules and fruit availability.

Incorrect Addresses / Reroutes

If an address change is needed after shipment, a Rs.1500 rerouting fee applies. Please contact customer support immediately.

Signatures

While signatures are not generally required, some deliveries (like offices or apartments) may require one as per the carrier’s policy.

Failed Delivery Attempts

If the courier makes three unsuccessful attempts, the gift will return to Akhrot. Failed deliveries are not eligible for refunds or replacements.

Delivery to Remote Areas

Please allow up to three additional business days for deliveries to remote or rural areas within Gilgit-Baltistan and Pakistan.

Restrictions

We cannot ship outside Pakistan or to P.O. Boxes and APO/FPO addresses. Certain perishable products may not be shipped to areas with agricultural restrictions.

Return and Substitution Policies

If your order arrives damaged or incorrect, please contact us within 24 hours of delivery with photo evidence. We’ll evaluate and offer a replacement or credit where applicable.

Substitutions may occur depending on seasonal availability, but we ensure equal or greater product value in all cases.

Due to the perishable nature of our products, we cannot accept returns once the product has been delivered successfully.
